Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance

Book By Robert M. Pirsig

This intriguing book blends a philosophical journey with a literal motorcycle road trip. So far, the narrative has introduced us to the narrator and his son, Chris, as they travel across the Midwest. The story delves into discussions on maintaining a motorcycle as a metaphor for navigating the complexities of life. The author reflects on "quality" and our understanding of underlying technology and values. It’s a profound exploration of how we connect with technology and the world around us, with deeper philosophical insights gradually unfolding.

Batman: A Death in The Family

This has been an incredible read so far! It's full of twists and turns; I've found myself exclaiming "no!" and gasping multiple times, which is rare for me as I usually pick up on plot points easily. Featuring some of the best writers and artists from the '80s and '90s, this classic truly delivers. Additionally, this version of the trade paperback includes several follow-up issues depicting Batman's grieving and recovery after the loss of his ward.
